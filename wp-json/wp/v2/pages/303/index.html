        • Prior authorization is required for all returns. Customers must contact our customer service to obtain a Return Authorization (+2768 095 9232) number before shipping the product back.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

          Original Packaging:<\/strong><\/em><\/p>\n

            \n
          • Products must be returned in their original packaging, including all accessories, manuals, and any other items that came with the product.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

            Unused and Undamaged:<\/strong><\/em><\/p>\n

              \n
            • Returned products must be unused, undamaged, and in the same condition as when they were received by the customer.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

              REFUND PROCESS<\/strong><\/p>\n

              Inspection:<\/strong><\/em><\/p>\n

                \n
              • All returned products will undergo a thorough inspection to ensure they meet the eligibility criteria outlined in this policy.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

                Refund Approval:<\/strong><\/em><\/p>\n

                  \n
                • Upon approval of the returned item, we will initiate the refund process. Refunds will be issued to the original method of payment.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

                  Refund Timeframe:<\/strong><\/em><\/p>\n

                    \n
                  • Refunds will be processed within 14 days of receiving the returned product.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

                        \n
